About

NOMADsignal is a recording artist, composer, and DJ focused on dramatic, cinematic electronic music shaped by melodic structure and atmospheric design.

Releases include EPs on Coldharbour Recordings (Black imprint), official remixes with Genix for Anjunabeats, and the full-length artist album The Great Journey. NOMADsignal has been featured on the In Search of Sunrise compilation series for three consecutive editions (16–18).

His music has received international radio support through A State of Trance, Global DJ Broadcast, and Vonyc Sessions, alongside multiple features on Above & Beyond’s Group Therapy, and has been played in live sets by established touring DJs across international club and festival stages.

Live appearances have included performances at Avalon Hollywood, Groove Cruise, Coldharbour label events, and curated club residencies.
